# MemoryLane: A Premium Digital Time Capsule 📸

MemoryLane is a sophisticated, full-stack application designed to preserve, organize, and share life's most precious moments with unparalleled elegance and privacy.

### 🌟 Core Highlights
- **Stunning UI/UX:** A high-end experience featuring glassmorphism, fluid animations (Framer Motion), and a premium dark-themed design system.
- **Multimedia Storytelling:** Capture life in high definition with support for photos, 4K videos, and high-fidelity voice notes.
- **Privacy First:** Granular visibility controls (Private, Friends Only, Public) ensuring you own your data and who sees it.
- **Social Ecosystem:** Build a trusted "Social Circle" to share exclusive memories and collaborate on shared albums.
- **Data Sovereignty:** Built-in tools to export your entire history—media and all—in a secure ZIP archive.

### 🛠️ Professional Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React (Vite), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Lucide Icons.
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js (RESTful API), JWT Security.
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) with a normalized relational schema.
- **Media Engine:** Cloudinary API for high-performance multimedia storage and delivery.
